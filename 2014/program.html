    Program

    Thursday, October 9th

    18:30-20:00: Informal Meet and Greet: Mar y Sol. Spanish restaurant in walking distance to the hotels and workshop venue. (Google Maps)

    Friday, October 10th

    TEL Auditorium 20th floor,
    TU Berlin,
    Ernst-Reuter-Platz 7,
    10587 Berlin, Germany

    Note: Presentation time for full research papers is 20 min and presentation time for work-in-progress papers are 15min.

    8:30-9:00 Registration

    9:00-9:10 Introduction and welcome to the EOOLT Workshop 2014
    Peter Pepper and David Broman

    09:10-10:40: Session I: Algorithms and Methods
    Session chair: Dirk Zimmer
    • Reshuffling: A Symbolic Pre-processing Algorithm for Improved Robustness, Performance and Parallelization for the Simulation of Differential Algebraic Equations (Full paper)
      Volker Waurich, Ines Gubsch, Christian Schubert, and Marcus Walther
    • Practical Realization and Adaptation of Cellier's Tearing Method (Full paper)
      Patrick Täuber, Lennart Ochel, Willi Braun, Bernhard Bachmann
    • Improving Efficiency of Hybrid System Simulation in Modelica (Full paper)
      Victorino Sanz, Alfonso Urquia, and Francesco Casella

    10:40-11:30: Coffee Break

    11:30-13:00: Session II: Modeling, Languages, and Compilation
    Session chair: François Cellier

    • Handling Infinitely Fast Processes in Continuous System Modeling (WiP paper)
      Dirk Zimmer
    • Towards Utilizing Repeating Structures for Constant Time Compilation of Large Modelica Models (WiP paper)
      Matthias Arzt, Volker Waurich, Jörg Welsch
    • Declarative Modelling for Bayesian Inference by Shallow Embedding (WiP paper)
      Henrik Nilsson and Thomas A. Nielsen
    • Simulation of Holonomic Mechanical Systems by means of Automatic Differentiation (WiP paper)
      Klaus Röbenack, Jan Winkler, Mirko Franke

    13:00-14:30: Lunch

    14:30 - 15:45: Session III: Parallelization and Simulation Performance
    Session chair: Henrik Nilsson

    • Automatic Task Based Analysis and Parallelization in the Context of Equation Based Languages (WiP paper)
      Mahder Gebremedhin and Peter Fritzson
    • Performance Analysis of Equation Based Simulations (WiP paper)
      Marcus Walther, Martin Flehmig, Ulf Markwardt, Niklas Worschech, Volker Waurich, and Christian Schubert
    • An Explicit Method for Decoupled Distributed Solvers in an Equation-Based Modelling Language (Full paper)
      Robert Braun and Petter Krus

    15:45-16:15: Coffee Break

    16:15-17:30 Session IV: Model Processing and Simulation
    Session chair: Peter Fritzson

    • Using Multi-Objective Optimization to Balance System-Level Model Complexity (Full paper)
      Alexander Pollok and Daniel Bender
    • Multi-Rate Integration Algorithms: a Promising Path for the Efficient Simulation of Object-Oriented Models of Very Large Systems (WiP paper)
      Akshay Ranade and Francesco Casella
    • Extraction of System Functions from 1-D Simulation Component Libraries (Full paper)
      Arquimedes Canedo and Jan H. Richter

    17:30-18:00: Concluding Discussion and Future Directions.
    19:00-??: Dinner at Lins Mandarin. The bus M45 leaves from Ernst-Reuter-Platz and stops at the restaurant (Google Maps).
