    September 10, 2025
    The next EOOLT workshop will take place in Bielefeld, Germany, on June 15, 2026.

    Registration

    For registration please use the following webpage:

    https://www.hsbi.de/ium/eoolt-registration

    Please note,

    • registration is mandatory and obligatory for all participants
    • for each paper, at least one presenter needs to participate
    • the registration fee is 120,-€ for regular participants and 30,-€ for students
    • please use the registration webpage to book your participation
    • the optional pre-meeting on Sunday, June 14, 2026, needs to be booked during registration and is paid separately
    • the optional post-meeting on Monday, June 15, 2026, after the workshop, also needs to be booked during registration and is paid separately

    Please register early to help us plan the workshop and the social events.

    Venue

    The EOOLT workshop is hosted by Hochschule Bielefeld (HSBI).

    The workshop venue is the conference area of the HSBI main building, Interaktion 1, 33619 Bielefeld.
    The site can be reached by Stadtbahn line 4, direction Lohmannshof, exit at Wellensiek. It is also served by bus line 31, stop Hochschule (HSBI).

    Information on the location and how to get there is found here.

    Social Program

    There will be the possibility to attend an optional pre-meeting on Sunday, June 14, before the workshop, and an optional post-meeting on Monday, June 15, after the workshop.
    Both events need to be booked during registration and will be charged separately.

    On Sunday, June 14, 19:00, we will meet at Brauhaus Joh. Albrecht, Hagenbruchstraße 8, 33602 Bielefeld.

    On Monday, June 15, 19:30, after the workshop, we will meet at The Bernstein, Niederwall 2, 33602 Bielefeld.
